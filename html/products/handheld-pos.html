Handheld POS

Mobile point-of-sale devices that enable your staff to process transactions anywhere in the store, improving customer service and reducing checkout wait times.

Checkout Anywhere, Anytime

Handheld POS devices revolutionize the retail checkout experience by bringing the register directly to the customer. These compact, powerful devices combine the functionality of a full POS system with the mobility needed for modern retail operations.

Equipped with high-performance barcode scanners, payment card readers, and receipt printers, handheld POS devices enable staff to complete entire transactions on the sales floor. This eliminates the need for customers to wait in line and creates opportunities for upselling and personalized service.

With wireless connectivity and battery-powered operation, these devices maintain connection to your central POS system while providing the flexibility to process transactions during busy periods, special events, or in areas without fixed checkout infrastructure.
