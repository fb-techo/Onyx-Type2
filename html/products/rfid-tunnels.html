Automated Bulk RFID Scanning

RFID tunnels are high-throughput scanning systems designed for automated bulk reading of RFID-tagged items. These systems enable rapid processing of large quantities of merchandise without manual scanning, dramatically reducing processing time and labor requirements.

Typically installed at receiving docks, shipping areas, or inventory staging locations, RFID tunnels use multiple antennas arranged in an enclosed configuration to ensure comprehensive tag reading. Items pass through the tunnel on conveyors, carts, or hand trucks, and all RFID tags are automatically read and recorded.

With advanced filtering algorithms and read validation, these systems provide accurate inventory counts even when tags are obscured or items are densely packed. Integration with warehouse management and inventory systems enables real-time updates and automated discrepancy detection.
