AI-Powered Weighing Scales

Intelligent weighing systems with AI recognition technology for fruit and vegetable, meat, fish, and roastery sections, enabling automatic product identification and accurate pricing.

Intelligent Product Recognition

AI-powered weighing scales combine precision weighing technology with advanced artificial intelligence to automatically identify products by visual recognition. These intelligent systems eliminate the need for manual PLU entry or barcode scanning, streamlining checkout processes in fresh food sections.

Using computer vision and machine learning algorithms, the scales analyze product appearance, shape, size, and color to identify items instantly. This technology is particularly valuable in departments where products are sold by weight and may not have barcodes, such as fresh produce, meat, seafood, and bakery items.

The AI recognition system learns and improves over time, becoming more accurate with continued use. Integrated with your POS system, these scales automatically retrieve pricing information, calculate total costs, and process transactions seamlessly, reducing errors and improving checkout speed.
