Fixed Counter Scanning

Bed scanners are fixed barcode scanning devices embedded in the checkout counter surface, allowing cashiers to scan products by simply passing them over the scanner window. This hands-free scanning approach speeds up transaction processing and reduces repetitive motion for checkout staff.

The scanners are designed to read barcodes from any angle, providing flexibility in how products are presented for scanning. Multiple scan lines and advanced decoding technology ensure fast, accurate barcode reading even for damaged or poorly printed barcodes.

Integrated seamlessly into POS systems, bed scanners provide instant product identification and pricing information. The fixed installation eliminates the need for handheld devices during checkout, creating a more streamlined and efficient transaction process while reducing the risk of scanner damage or misplacement.
