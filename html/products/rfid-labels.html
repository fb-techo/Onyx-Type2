RFID Labels

Intelligent labels that combine RFID tag encoding with barcode printing capabilities, enabling automated identification and tracking while maintaining visual product information.

Dual Technology Labeling

RFID labels integrate RFID inlay technology with printable surfaces, allowing retailers to encode RFID tags and print barcodes, text, and graphics on the same label. This dual-technology approach enables both automated RFID tracking and traditional barcode scanning, providing maximum flexibility and compatibility.

The labels contain embedded RFID chips and antennas that can be encoded with unique identification data, enabling bulk reading, long-range scanning, and automated inventory tracking. Simultaneously, the label surface supports thermal printing for barcodes, product information, and visual identification.

Compatible with RFID encoding printers, these labels can be printed and encoded in a single pass, streamlining the labeling process. The combination of RFID and barcode technologies ensures compatibility with existing systems while enabling advanced tracking capabilities for inventory management and loss prevention.
