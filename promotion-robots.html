Promotion Robots

Autonomous robots that navigate store aisles to display promotions, provide customer assistance, and collect valuable analytics data while enhancing the shopping experience.

Autonomous Store Engagement

Promotion robots are autonomous mobile platforms equipped with displays, sensors, and navigation technology that move throughout retail spaces to engage customers, display promotions, and provide assistance. These innovative devices bring marketing messages directly to customers and can answer questions, provide directions, and showcase products.

Using advanced navigation and obstacle avoidance systems, these robots safely navigate store aisles, interact with customers, and return to charging stations autonomously. Integrated displays and speakers enable dynamic promotion delivery, while sensors and cameras provide valuable analytics on customer movement, engagement, and store traffic patterns.

The robots can be programmed with store layouts, product locations, and promotional content, enabling them to guide customers to specific items, highlight special offers, and provide general store information. Integration with inventory and POS systems allows them to provide real-time product availability and pricing information.
