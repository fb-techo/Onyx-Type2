Handheld Scanner

Portable barcode scanners for POS terminals, providing flexible scanning capabilities for products that cannot be passed over fixed scanners or for additional checkout scanning needs.

Portable Scanning Flexibility

Handheld scanners are portable barcode scanning devices used as accessories with POS systems, providing flexibility for scanning products that are too large, too heavy, or inconvenient to pass over fixed bed scanners. These scanners offer the mobility needed for various checkout scenarios.

Available in corded and cordless models, handheld scanners provide reliable barcode reading with ergonomic designs for comfortable extended use. They complement fixed bed scanners by handling edge cases where fixed scanning is impractical, such as large items, items on shopping carts, or products with barcodes in difficult positions.

Integrated with POS systems via USB or wireless connectivity, handheld scanners provide instant product identification and seamlessly integrate into checkout workflows. Their portability makes them ideal backup scanning solutions and essential accessories for comprehensive POS configurations.
