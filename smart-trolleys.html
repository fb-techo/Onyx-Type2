Smart Trolleys

Shopping carts with integrated screens and technology that provide navigation, product information, self-checkout capabilities, and personalized shopping experiences for enhanced customer engagement.

Interactive Shopping Experience

Smart trolleys transform the traditional shopping cart into an interactive shopping assistant, featuring integrated tablets or displays that provide customers with navigation, product information, promotional offers, and self-checkout capabilities. These advanced carts enhance the shopping experience while providing retailers with valuable customer engagement and analytics data.

The integrated displays can show store maps, help customers locate products, display personalized offers based on shopping patterns, and enable self-scanning and checkout directly from the cart. This reduces wait times at traditional checkout lanes and creates a more convenient shopping experience.

With built-in scales, barcode scanners, and payment processing capabilities, smart trolleys enable customers to scan items as they shop, track their spending, and complete their purchase without waiting in checkout lines. The technology also provides retailers with insights into shopping patterns, product interactions, and customer behavior.
