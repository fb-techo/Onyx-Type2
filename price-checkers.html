Self-Service Product Information

Price checkers are standalone kiosks placed throughout the store that allow customers to independently look up product information, prices, and availability by scanning barcodes. These devices empower customers to find information quickly without waiting for staff assistance, improving the shopping experience while freeing staff for other tasks.

Equipped with barcode scanners, touchscreen displays, and connectivity to your inventory and pricing systems, price checkers provide real-time product information. They can display current prices, product descriptions, nutritional information, ingredient lists, and stock availability, depending on your product data and requirements.

Modern price checkers feature intuitive interfaces designed for ease of use by customers of all ages and technical abilities. Some models also support additional features such as store maps, promotional information, and loyalty program integration, making them valuable tools for customer engagement beyond simple price checking.
