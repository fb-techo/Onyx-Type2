Handheld PDTs

Phone and gun-style portable data terminals that combine scanning, computing, and connectivity capabilities for efficient inventory management and retail operations.

Mobile Inventory Management

Handheld Portable Data Terminals (PDTs) are essential tools for modern retail operations, providing staff with powerful mobile computing capabilities for inventory management, price checking, and sales floor activities. Available in both phone-style and pistol-grip configurations, these devices adapt to your operational needs.

Equipped with high-performance barcode and RFID scanners, robust wireless connectivity, and enterprise-grade operating systems, PDTs enable real-time inventory updates, product lookups, and transaction processing directly from the sales floor or warehouse.

With durable construction designed for retail environments, long battery life, and intuitive interfaces, these devices empower your team to work efficiently while maintaining connection to your central systems for accurate, up-to-date information.
