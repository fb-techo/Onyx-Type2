Electronic Shelf Labels

Wireless digital price tags that replace traditional paper labels, enabling instant price updates, reducing labor costs, and improving pricing accuracy across your entire store.

Digital Price Management

Electronic Shelf Labels (ESLs) transform traditional pricing management by replacing paper labels with digital displays that can be updated instantly from a central system. These wireless devices display prices, product information, and promotional messages while eliminating the time-consuming and error-prone process of manual label changes.

Our ESL solution uses low-power wireless technology to communicate with a central management system, enabling store-wide price updates in minutes rather than hours. The displays remain visible for extended periods on minimal battery power, typically lasting 5-7 years before replacement.

With support for various display sizes and formats, including full-color options, ESLs can display prices, product details, QR codes, and promotional graphics. Integration with POS and inventory systems ensures consistency between shelf prices and checkout prices, improving customer trust and reducing pricing errors.
