Customer Viewing Screen

Customer-facing displays that show transaction details, item prices, and purchase totals, providing transparency and enabling customers to verify their transactions during checkout.

Transparent Transaction Display

Customer viewing screens are displays positioned to face customers at checkout counters, showing real-time transaction information including item descriptions, prices, quantities, and running totals. These screens enhance customer experience by providing transparency and enabling customers to verify their purchases during checkout.

Connected to POS systems, customer viewing screens automatically display transaction details as items are scanned, providing immediate visual feedback. The displays show product information, pricing, discounts, taxes, and final totals, allowing customers to confirm accuracy before payment.

With clear, easy-to-read displays and automatic updates, customer viewing screens reduce pricing disputes, improve customer confidence, and enhance the checkout experience. Some models also support promotional messaging and store information, providing additional value beyond transaction display.
