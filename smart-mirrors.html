Smart Mirrors

Interactive fitting room technology that enhances the try-on experience with virtual styling, size recommendations, product information, and easy assistance requests.

Next-Generation Fitting Room Experience

Smart mirrors combine traditional mirrors with interactive display technology, creating an enhanced fitting room experience that helps customers make purchase decisions while providing retailers with valuable engagement data. These mirrors feature touchscreen overlays or embedded displays that transform the fitting room into an interactive shopping assistant.

The technology enables customers to request different sizes or colors, view product details, see styling suggestions, and request assistance from sales staffâ€”all without leaving the fitting room. Some systems also offer virtual try-on capabilities, allowing customers to see how items look in different colors or styles without physically changing.

Integration with inventory systems ensures customers can see real-time availability of sizes and colors, reducing the need for staff to check stock. The mirrors also provide retailers with insights into which items are being tried on most frequently, helping optimize inventory and merchandising decisions.
