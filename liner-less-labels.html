Liner-less Labels

Eco-friendly labeling solutions that eliminate backing paper waste, reduce environmental impact, and provide cost savings while maintaining professional print quality.

Sustainable Labeling Technology

Liner-less labels represent an innovative approach to labeling that eliminates the need for backing paper, reducing waste and environmental impact. These labels use special adhesive technology that allows labels to be wound without a liner, significantly reducing material usage and disposal requirements.

By removing the liner material, retailers can reduce label waste by up to 50%, lower material costs, and minimize environmental impact. The labels maintain excellent adhesion properties and print quality while providing the same functionality as traditional labels.

Compatible with thermal printers designed for liner-less technology, these labels are ideal for applications where sustainability is a priority. They work particularly well for shipping labels, product tags, and other applications where traditional label backing would be discarded after use.
