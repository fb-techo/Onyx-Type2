People Counters

Foot traffic analytics systems that accurately count and analyze customer traffic patterns, providing insights for staffing optimization, store layout improvements, and conversion rate analysis.

Comprehensive Traffic Analytics

People counting systems use advanced sensors and analytics technology to accurately track customer traffic entering and exiting retail locations. These systems provide real-time and historical data on foot traffic patterns, enabling retailers to make data-driven decisions about staffing, store operations, and marketing effectiveness.

The counting technology uses various sensor types including overhead sensors, thermal imaging, video analytics, and Wi-Fi tracking to accurately count customers while maintaining privacy. The systems can distinguish between entries and exits, track peak traffic times, and provide detailed analytics on traffic patterns throughout the day, week, and year.

By combining traffic data with sales data, retailers can calculate conversion rates—the percentage of visitors who make a purchase—helping identify opportunities to improve sales performance. The analytics also enable optimization of staffing schedules based on traffic patterns, ensuring adequate coverage during peak times while controlling labor costs.
